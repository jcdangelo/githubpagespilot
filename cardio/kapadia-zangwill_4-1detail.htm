Subendocardial Infarct

Subendocardial acute myocardial infarction (yellow arrow).  The rest of the myocardium is normal (marked by stars) and includes two thirds of the wall along the epicardial portion adjacent to the infarct.  The infarted tissue is surrounded by a thin hyperemic rim (blue arrows).  Compare this with the transmural myocardial infarction image.
